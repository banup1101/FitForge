Understand Your Body Type

Ectomorph

Ectomorphs are characterized by a lean and slender build, with difficulty gaining muscle and weight. They tend to have fast metabolisms.

Mesomorph

Mesomorphs have a naturally muscular and athletic build, with a moderate metabolism. They can gain muscle relatively easily and maintain a balanced physique.

Endomorph

Endomorphs typically have a softer, rounder physique with a higher tendency to store fat. They may find it challenging to lose weight but can often build muscle effectively.
